About | Heroes of Hope

Heroes of hope is run by 3 siblings and their mom to raise money for our baby sister. We sell greeting cards, birthday cards, all occasion cards and custom cards! We are always trying to come up with new card ideas. So far, we have raised over 3000 dollars for her intensive therapy. We also have given over 300 dollars to a charity called Starlight foundation.
